Locking without Signatures

In Bitcoin, a coin (UTXO) isn't "sent" to an address. Instead, it is locked behind a mathematical formula: OP_SHA256 <targetHash> OP_EQUAL.

To spend it, you don't need a signature or private key. Anyone who presents the correct string that hashes to that target hash can unlock it and pay it to any destination address they choose.
